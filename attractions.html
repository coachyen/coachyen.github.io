Greystone Golf Course

Playing to a Par of 72 and just over 7,200 yards from the championship tees, Greystone offers 4 sets of tees on every hole to ensure the course is playable, challenging, and rewarding for golfers of all skill levels. Simply put, this golf course layout is a must play for anyone in Western/Central New York. Rated 4.5 Stars by Golf Digest


Long Acre Farms


Spring and Summer are a lovely time to visit our farm! The ice-cream shop, farm market and back 40 activity area are open daily! Spend the afternoon playing, jumping and exploring our activity area and treat yourself to an ice-cream dessert. Long Acre Farms is most famously known for our Fall Festival Weekends which are Saturdays & Sundays from 10:00am - 6:00pm. We are home of NY's original Amazing Maize Maze, one of the longest running corn mazes in the nation.
